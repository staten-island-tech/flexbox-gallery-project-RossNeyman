Screwing Around

Torx Head

A good philips works well with properly formed screw drivers but for higher torque applications hex and torx bits are used. Hex bits wear and the screw heads are easily damaged. Torx bits are being used a lot by motorcycle and car manufacturers because they damage less easily are and are easier to drive.

Truss Head

The dome shape of the heads of truss screws is low enough to use for most projects, but an advantage is that the screws are difficult to remove or tamper with. Another advantage of a truss screw is that the wide head prevents it from sinking too deeply into the material and causing tearing.
