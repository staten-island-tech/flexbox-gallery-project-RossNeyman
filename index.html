Screwing Around

Bugle Head

A good philips works well with properly formed screw drivers but for higher torque applications hex and torx bits are used. Hex bits wear and the screw heads are easily damaged. Torx bits are being used a lot by motorcycle and car manufacturers because they damage less easily are and are easier to drive.
